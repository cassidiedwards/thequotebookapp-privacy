Privacy Policy

The Quote Book App

This privacy policy explains how The Quote Book App handles user data.

Data Collection

The Quote Book App does not collect personal information for advertising, marketing, or tracking purposes.

Any content created within the app, such as quotes, authors, notes, and related metadata, is provided directly by the user.

Data Storage

Data created in The Quote Book App is stored securely using Apple CloudKit and iCloud services.

  • Data is associated with the user’s Apple ID
  • Data is only accessible to the app and authorized users
  • Apple manages authentication, storage, and security

Data Sharing

The Quote Book App does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Data may be shared only with other users explicitly invited by the user through app features.

Third-Party Services

The Quote Book App relies on Apple’s CloudKit and iCloud infrastructure. No other third-party analytics or advertising services are used.

User Control

Users can manage their data through the app and through iOS iCloud settings.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as the app evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
