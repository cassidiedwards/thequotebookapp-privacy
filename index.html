# Privacy Policy The Quote Book App respects your privacy. ## Data Collection This app does not collect personal data for advertising or tracking purposes. Quotes and related content are stored securely using Apple CloudKit and are only accessible to the app’s users. ## Data Sharing No data is shared with third parties. ## Data Storage All data is stored in Apple’s iCloud infrastructure and follows Apple’s security and privacy standards. ## Contact If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: cassidi@example.com
